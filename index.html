Introduction

My name is JOSEPH MUTYABA.
I'm a Ugandan, male and a resident in Kampala the capital of Uganda. The earliest years of my life were spent in a Village in Lwengo district called Kabulasokke. While there, i was roomed and raised up as a stron and cradle Catholic, with strict adherence to the teachin of our Lord Jesus Christ.

It was in this very place that I received the three reat sacramnts of Baptism, Penance and my first "Holy Communion."
After this, at the age of ten, I was brought by mum to come and stay in Kampala. In total Ispent 9 years in Lwengo and the rest in different parts of Kampala.

Academic history

I started my studies at a rural school called "Namunye Bright Academy", then I transferred to "St. Mary's Primary School", then to "Good Shepherd Primary School". From there I transferred to "Trinity Children's centre" and finally to "Rock Foundation Primary School" from where I sat for my "Primary Leaving Examinations" in the year 2014.

I joined Kyambogo College High School which for six years I fully attended. During the fourth year of my college life that is 2018, I sat for my "Uganda Certificate of Education" examinations.
In 2021, I sat for my "Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education" examinations in the same college.

I was admitted on National merit to attend Makerere University, the greatest and mightiest hiher learning institution in the country. At the time of writin this document, I was persuing a Bachelor of Science Degree in Software Enineering in the aforementioned Univeesity and in year one.
