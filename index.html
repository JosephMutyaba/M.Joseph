Introduction

My name is JOSEPH MUTYABA.
I'm a Ugandan, male and a resident in Kampala the capital of Uganda.
The earliest years of my life were spent in a Village in Lwengo district called Kabulasokke. While there, i was groomed and raised up as a strong and cradle Catholic, with strict adherence to the
teaching of our Lord Jesus Christ.

It was in this very place that I received the three reat sacramnts of Baptism, Penance and my first
"Holy Communion."
After this, at the age of ten, I mum took me to stay with her in Kampala.
In total I spent 9 years in Lwengo and the rest in different parts of Kampala.

Academic history

I started my studies at a rural school called "Namunye Bright Academy", then I transferred to "St. Mary's Primary School", then to "Good Shepherd Primary School". From there I transferred to "Trinity Children's centre" and finally to "Rock Foundation Primary School" from where I sat for my Primary Leaving Examinations in the year 2014.

I joined Kyambogo College High School which for six years I fully attended. During the fourth
year of my college life that is 2018, I sat for my "Uganda Certificate of Education" examinations.
In 2021, I sat for my "Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education" examinations in the same college.

I was admitted on National merit to attend Makerere University, the greatest and mightiest
higher learning institution in the country. At the time of writing this document, I was persuing
a Bachelor of Science Degree in Software Engineering in the aforementioned University and in year one.

All these achievements are the result of the infalible communion between a creature and its Creator
in which case the creature is I myself Joseph Kevin Mutyaba and the "Creator" is the "The Lord
God" who is the "Almighty and Eternity"
